# 🛒 AI Abandoned Cart Orchestrator

**Recover lost revenue with intelligent, RAG-driven customer re-engagement.**

---

## 📉 The Business Problem
E-commerce businesses lose an average of **70% of potential revenue** to abandoned carts. Generic email reminders are often ignored, leading to low conversion rates and wasted marketing spend.

## 💡 The Solution
This orchestrator uses **Agentic RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** to analyze customer behavior and product data, generating hyper-personalized re-engagement workflows that feel human and high-value.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
- 🤖 **Agentic Reasoning**: Analyzes *why* a customer might have left (price, intent, friction).
- 🧠 **RAG Integration**: Fetches real-time product benefits and inventory status to create urgency.
- ⚡ **Autonomous Workflows**: Built on **n8n** for low-latency, scalable execution.
- 📈 **Outcome-Focused**: Designed to increase cart recovery rates by up to 25%.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
- **Orchestration**: n8n
- **Intelligence**: OpenAI / Claude via Agentic Workflows
- **Data Layer**: JSON-LD / Product Feed RAG
- **Security**: Gitleaks-hardened environment

---

## 🛠️ Quick Start
1. Import the  workflow into your n8n instance.
2. Configure your LLM credentials.
3. Connect your e-commerce platform (Shopify/WooCommerce) via Webhooks.
